KRI Name

Economic Value at Risk (+200 bps instantaneous and parallel shock)

Description

EVE risk is a traditional industry metric for economic value at risk for banks. It measures the variability of fair value of the bank's interest rate sensitive positions to various interest rate shocks. In this particular case, it is the variability of EVE under a 200 bps parallel and instantaneous shock scenario.

Formula

Absolute Value of (EVE for 200 bps shock - EVE for Base Case)
